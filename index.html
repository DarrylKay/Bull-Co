ABOUT

Bull & Co.

'As a client, you are our number one priority. Bull & Co offers an opportunity for our clients to relax and enjoy their surroundings while experiencing a customized level of service. Together our barbers have over 35 years of experience and use modern-day cutting edge grooming techniques with timeless traditional services. Our aim is to provide a quality customer experience while constantly pushing new levels of innotive grooming styles. Situated within our premises we have dedicated facilities for our younger clients as we can appreciate that not every child likes to be in a busy room with the stress of having a haircut.'
